// 01 — About Us

Where Engineering Meets Elite Ambition

Ctrl Alt Elite is a competitive FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team based at American High School, Fremont CA. We build robots, write code, and push the boundaries of what a student-led engineering team can achieve.

Our team combines hardware engineering, software development, and community outreach to compete at the highest level while making a lasting impact on our school and surrounding community.

As a first-year team, we're building our legacy from the ground up — recruiting the sharpest minds at AHS and competing with the intensity of veterans.

// 08 — The Process

How FTC Works

FIRST Tech Challenge is a robotics competition where teams of students design, build, and program robots to compete in an alliance-based game. It's about more than winning — it's about learning engineering, teamwork, and leadership that lasts a lifetime.

01
Design
Brainstorm mechanisms, create CAD models, plan strategy for the season game.
▼
02
Build
Assemble the robot, wire electronics, test each subsystem independently.
▼
03
Code
Write autonomous routines, teleop controls, and computer vision pipelines.
▼
04
Compete
Alliance matches, judging interviews, and awards across regional and state events.
